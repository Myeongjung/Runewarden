How to Play

  • Summon cards — Click card, then click an empty hex to place a tower.
  • Augment cards — Click card, then click an existing tower to upgrade it.
  • Spell cards — Click card to activate instantly.
  • Towers attack enemies automatically. Don't let enemies reach the exit!
  • You have Nexus HP. Lose them all and the run ends.
  • ⚡ Enrage — some enemies speed up dramatically at low HP. Burst them down!
  • 🛡️ Damage Reduction — Plague Carriers & Phantoms take less damage. Use multiple towers.
  • 🧊 Slow Immunity — Juggernauts & Siege Beasts cannot be slowed. Focus raw damage.
